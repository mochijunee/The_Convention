The Declaration of Sentiments

When: July 20

This event will take place with both men and women present. It will be drafted by the organizers of the convention, like Elizabeth Cady Stanton (see the "About" page for the rest of the organizers). Modeled after the Declaration of Independence, it will lay out the foundations for woman's rights and expose the grievances women experience as a result of male supremacy.
